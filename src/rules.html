Game Features:

The Game has following features:

Weapons

There are a total of 4 different weapons placed on random spots on the Grid. The weapons and the damage they do are as follows:

  1. Stone (default): This is a default weapon that both players start with. Damage: -5
  2. dagger Damage: -10
  3. Pistol Damage: -15
  4. Grenade Damage: -20
  5. Rocket Damage: -35

Rules

The Rules of the game are quite simple.

  • There is a 10x10 Grid for player movements.
  • There are a total of 10 obstacles and 4 different weapons, randomly placed on the grid.
  • The players can move freely in all four directions (up,down,right,left) except diagonally.
  • If there are any obstacles in the way of the player, it can not move past the obstacle and the movements would be restricted.
  • If the player moves to the tile, where a weapon is placed, the player's weapon will be swapped.
  • If both the players come next to each other, the fight will start!
